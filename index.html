About Buried Games Studio

Buried Games Studio is a Kuwait-based indie game studio driven by creativity and technical excellence. Our team is composed of passionate developers, designers, and storytellers who strive to craft immersive, unforgettable gaming experiences.

We specialize in both 2D and 3D development using industry-leading engines like Unity and Unreal Engine, tailoring each project to achieve the perfect balance of playability and innovation.

Studio founder: Fahed Alahmad — software engineer, game designer, and entrepreneur.
