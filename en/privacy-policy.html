Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2025

Buried Games Studio (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or interact with our games.

1. Information We Collect

  • Automatically collected: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited (via tools like Google Analytics).
  • Voluntarily provided: Name, email, or contact form details.
  • Third-party services: We may use APIs or embeds from platforms like YouTube or Firebase, which may collect additional information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To improve user experience and optimize our website.
  • To respond to your inquiries or support requests.
  • To showcase game content via platforms like YouTube or social media.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and performance. You can disable cookies via your browser settings.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. Some data may be shared with third-party services strictly to operate or analyze the site.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard methods to protect your information.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us if you have such a request.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions, email us at contact@buriedgames.com.
